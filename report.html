Key Insights & Observations

Strategy Evolution

V4 achieved the best risk-adjusted performance with PF 0.85 and smallest loss (₹-7,343). Progressive improvements in exit conditions and regime filtering reduced losses by 41% from v3 to v4.

ML Enhancement

The GBM classifier improved train-set win rate to 46.2% but showed overfitting with test-set drop to 25%. The threshold of 0.602 filtered aggressively, reducing trade count but not improving out-of-sample P&L.

Exit Analysis

Theta bleed dominates exits (72% in V4), indicating most straddles decay before reaching profit targets. Vega wins account for 22-28% of exits — the primary profit driver when IV drops after entry.

Challenges

Consistent negative P&L across versions suggests structural headwinds: transaction costs (₹120/trade), asymmetric risk from gamma exposure, and the challenge of timing IV compression entries.
